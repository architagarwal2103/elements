We're more than just a group of chapters..

While SE chapters are great, we offer even more beyond the local/school level. With SE events and resources you have the opportunity to gain invaluable knowledge and skills, meet professionals in STEM fields, network with students across the world, and jumpstart your career.

SE Chapters

STEM Explorations chapters are designed to help students launch stem clubs at their own schools. All of our registered chapters have access to a variety of opportunities like getting recognition from SE global, getting exclusive access to our some of our competition and events resources, startup support from HQ, and becoming part of a community of aspiring international STEM leaders and innovators.
